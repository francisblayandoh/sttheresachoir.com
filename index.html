About Us

Who We Are

The Choir is a vibrant and dedicated Catholic church choir founded in the early 1970s. Over the decades, we have grown in faith and commitment to the service of God and the church community. Our music ministry is deeply rooted in Catholic traditions, bringing life to liturgical celebrations and other church activities through hymns and sacred music.

Our Team Number of Members: Currently, the choir comprises 30 committed members, including sopranos, altos, tenors, and bass voices.

Choirmasters: We are guided by two(2) talented choirmasters MR FRANCIS BLAY ANDOH & MR. ANTHONY COLE and one Conductor Mrs. Cecilia Tuba Nda, who bring their expertise and passion for music to lead our rehearsals and performances.

Instrumentalists: Our team is supported by two(2) skilled instrumentalists Mr. FRANCIS BLAY ANDOH(keyboardist) & Mr. CHRISTOPHER AKAWATSON(Trumpeter) who enhance our music with instruments such as the organ,keyborad, drums and brass.

Our Patrons The choir is blessed with the support and guidance of 10 patrons including our vibrant Catechist MR.LAWRENCE BLAY, who play a crucial role in providing mentorship and ensuring the spiritual and organizational growth of the choir.

Our Mission

Our mission is to glorify God through sacred music, uplift the congregation's spirit, and foster a deeper connection to the liturgy. We strive to inspire worship through harmonious singing and a commitment to excellence in our ministry. We invite all members of the community and beyond to join us in our journey of faith and music. Whether as a member of the choir or a supporter, your participation enriches our ministry and helps us fulfill our calling.
